Knowledge Base

Delivery FAQs

Our average delivery time is between 8-15 minutes depending on your distance from our nearest dark store. You can track the real-time location of your delivery partner via the 'Track Order' section in the app.
Once an order is packed (usually within 2 minutes), addresses cannot be changed to ensure fast delivery. If the rider hasn't left yet, you can contact Support via Live Chat to check for feasibility.

Payments & Refunds

Don't worry! If an order fails after deduction, the amount is automatically reversed by our bank within 2-4 hours. If it doesn't show up in 24 hours, please share the transaction ID via 'Raise a Complaint'.
